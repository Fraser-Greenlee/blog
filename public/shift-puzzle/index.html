How to play

Form the coloured shape shown under Make anywhere it fits on the board. When you do, those candies clear, you earn moves, and the Next shape slides in. It's endless — chase a high score.

The four edge arrows shift the whole board one step — candies that fall off one side wrap around to the other.

The four corner buttons shift the candies one step around each ring: the right corners move them clockwise (↻), the left corners anticlockwise (↺). The centre candy of the 3×3 stays put.

You have a limited number of moves: each move spends one and each shape you clear earns several back, so you have to keep clearing or the run ends. The move that reverses your last one is highlighted — tap it to undo (you get the move back); you can keep undoing back to your last clear, or Reset for a fresh run.

The + axis modes also require each candy's bar to lie the way the target shows — all upright or all sideways. A candy turns 90° only as it leaves a corner of its ring (sliding along a straight edge keeps its facing; the centre spins with every ring move). In the partial variant only the cells that show a bar are constrained — the rest take any facing.

The two shapes mode shows two different-colour shapes you must form on the board at the same time to clear together. Every target is built from the colours actually on your board, so it's always achievable.

Keyboard: arrows shift, Q/E ring-shift, R reset, N new board.
